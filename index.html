End of an Amazing Era

After serving communities across this great country for more than 30 years, it is with very mixed emotions that this chapter comes to an end and Network Communications is closing our doors.

From one phone store in Caloundra on the Sunshine coast back in 1992 we were able to expand to over 70 stores at our peak. The majority of these stores were locally owned and operated by some of the most incredible people, who in turn employed thousands of team members who were committed to loving our customers, to each of you who have been part of the journey thank you.

To our trusted partners it has been a privilege to work alongside you, with a special mention to Optus who without their support we wouldn't have been able to fulfil our vision. To the charities we have had the honour of supporting over the years, keep up the great work you are changing the world for the better.

Its been the adventure of a lifetime and it's with a heartfelt thankyou to every single customer who has made it possible. Rest assure all our stores are remaining open and continue to serve you as Optus licenced stores.

Network

Network Communications was officially formed in 1992 as one phone store in Caloundra, Sunshine Coast Qld. With a mandate to expand through strategic communities has seen the Network model grow to over 50 stores across Australia. It was at this time a new partnership was formed between Network Communications and Optus. 

Optus, the new kid on the block, was the challenger in the telecommunications industry and for more than 2 decades Network and Optus have helped people connect in the communities we serve. 

Network Communications is not like any other company you may have ever come across. On the surface we look like a business, however at our core we exist to love people. This is at the heart of everything we do.
